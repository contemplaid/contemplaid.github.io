About Us

Who we are

Well, we'd like to think we're relatively rational human beings, trying to live an examined, purposeful, and intentional life. Part of that, we believe, is sharing our learning and thoughts with other people who may also be interested in the same thing (as we understand that to be).

This is an attempt to document and share our learnings as we grow.

Our Philosophy

Socrates (supposedly) said (something along the lines of) the unexamined life is not worth living.

We think that is the only way to live a good, fulfilling life. That may seem like a bold to some, but if someone disagrees with it, would they not have already examined their life, before their disagreement?
