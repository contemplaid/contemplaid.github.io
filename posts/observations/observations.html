To step back a bit, our overall goal here is to figure out how to live a fulfilled, and happy life. The best way to achieve that is to come up with a philosophy of living that provides a good purpose, addresses injustice in the world, and provides support to bear through life's challenges. Here, I'm trying to provide a philosophy of living that has worked for me, a philosophy of living with a rational basis that can be defended (in my opinion) by logic, and provides a reasonable basis for the parts that require a suspension of disbelief.

As I mentioned in The Approach section of the About page, this whole idea begins with observations.

That's where we get to the "cautious" answers.
