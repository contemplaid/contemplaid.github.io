Surah Nahl - النحل

أَتَىٰٓ أَمْرُ ٱللَّهِ فَلَا تَسْتَعْجِلُوهُ ۚ سُبْحَٰنَهُۥ وَتَعَالَىٰ عَمَّا يُشْرِكُونَ

  • literal translation: The command of Allah has come, so do not hasten it. Exalted is He and high above what they associate with Him.
  • context: revelation during a time of opposition and disbelief from the Quraysh tribe
  • meaning: Allah's plan and decree will unfold in its own time, and His transcendence above any false deities

يُنَزِّلُ ٱلْمَلَـٰٓئِكَةَ بِٱلرُّوحِ مِنْ أَمْرِهِۦ عَلَىٰ مَن يَشَآءُ مِنْ عِبَادِهِۦٓ أَنْ أَنْذِرُوٓا۟ أَنَّهُۥ لَآ إِلَـٰهَ إِلَّآ أَنَا۠ فَٱتَّقُونِ

  • literal translation: He sends down the angels, with the inspiration of His command, upon whom He wills of His servants, [telling them], "Warn that there is no deity except Me; so fear Me."
  • context: emphasizes the role of angels as messengers of Allah's commands and warnings
  • meaning: the importance of monotheism and fearing Allah alone

خَلَقَ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضَ بِٱلْحَقِّ ۚ تَعَـٰلَىٰ عَمَّا يُشْرِكُونَ

  • literal translation: He created the heavens and the earth in truth. Exalted is He above what they associate with Him.
  • context: affirmation of Allah's creation of the universe as a sign of His power and authority
  • meaning: rejection of polytheism and false beliefs about creation

خَلَقَ ٱلْإِنسَـٰنَ مِن نُّطْفَةٍۢ فَإِذَا هُوَ خَصِيمٌۭ مُّبِينٌۭ
