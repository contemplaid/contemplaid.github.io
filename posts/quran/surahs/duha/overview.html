Surah Duha - الضحى

وَٱلضُّحَىٰ

  • literal translation: By the morning brightness
  • refers to the time of early morning when the sun is bright but there is no heat yet
  • also interpreted as the forenoon or the time after sunrise

وَٱلَّيْلِ إِذَا سَجَىٰ

  • literal translation: And by the night when it covers with darkness
  • refers to the time of night when things are not moving and there is stillness
  • سَجْ البَحْر
  • شَاةٌ سَجْوَاء

مَا وَدَّعَكَ رَبُّكَ وَمَا قَلَىٰ

  • literal translation: Your Lord has not forsaken you, nor has He detested (you)
  • context: reassurance to Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) during a period of distress and revelation pause
  • meaning: Allah's continuous support and care for the Prophet despite challenges
  • وَدِيعْ
  • مِقْلَاي

وَلَلْآخِرَةُ خَيْرٌ لَّكَ مِنَ الْأُولَىٰ

  • literal translation: And the Hereafter is better for you than the first (life)
  • context: encouragement to focus on the eternal rewards of the Hereafter rather than temporary worldly life
  • meaning: the trials and hardships of this life are insignificant compared to the eternal bliss of the Hereafter
